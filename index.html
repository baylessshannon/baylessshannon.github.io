About Me

Hello! I'm Shannon (she/her), an aspiring graphic designer based in Minneapolis, MN.

Much of my creative spark comes from my favorite musical artists and songs. Music has always been a profound influence on my life, and I believe that connecting with artists and their work through a visual medium can significantly enhance the overall listening experience. I love using bright colors and bold type in my work to convey my excitement from hearing my favorite songs or seeing my favoite bands live.

I am always eager to learn and grow as a designer, constantly seeking out new experiences and influences that can help shape and refine my personal style.

Feel free to reach out via instagram (@cool.shannnn) if you ever want to talk design, music, or favoire Minneapolis spots!
